## 5. MITIGATION OPTIONS

| Option | Implementation | Trade-off |
|--------|----------------|-----------|
| Reduce equity allocation | Trim highest-beta holdings, move to bonds/cash | Lower expected return in bull markets |
| Add bond buffer | Increase AGG/BND/Treasury allocation | Lower yield, but draws down less in crashes |
| Add international diversification | Increase VXUS or similar | Currency risk, but lower correlation to US drawdown |
| Add cash reserve | Hold 5-10% in cash/money market | Opportunity cost, but funding for rebalancing and emergencies |
| Hedge with options | Buy puts on broad index or expensive holdings | Ongoing premium cost, capped protection |
| Factor tilt adjustment | Reduce pure growth exposure, add value/quality | Lower upside in momentum markets |

---

## 6. QUESTIONS TO ANSWER

1. What is your maximum acceptable drawdown?
2. When will you need to withdraw from this portfolio?
3. Are you adding new capital, or is this allocation static?
4. Do you have other assets or income sources to absorb shocks?
5. Would you rebalance during a drawdown, or panic sell?
